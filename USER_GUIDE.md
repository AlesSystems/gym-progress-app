## ğŸ’¾ Workout Templates

### Saving a Workout as Template

1. **Start a workout** from the Dashboard
2. **Add exercises and sets** as you normally would
3. When finished, tap **"Finish"** button
4. In the dialog, choose **"Save as Template"**
5. **Enter a name** for your template (e.g., "Push Day", "Leg Day")
6. Tap **"Save"**
7. Confirmation appears - tap **"Finish Workout"**

Your template is now saved! ğŸ‰

### Starting a Workout from Template

1. On the Dashboard, tap **"Start Workout"**
2. If you have templates, you'll see a dialog:
   - **"New Workout"** - Start empty workout
   - **"Use Template"** - Choose from your saved templates
3. Tap **"Use Template"**
4. **Select a template** from the list
5. Your workout starts pre-filled with exercises and sets!

You can still:
- Add more exercises
- Delete exercises
- Modify sets
- Add/remove sets

### Viewing Your Templates

On the Dashboard, check **"Quick Stats"** card:
- It shows: `Templates: X` (where X is number of saved templates)
